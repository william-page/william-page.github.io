I am a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Physics department at UC Berkeley, where I work in the Pyle group and as a member of the SuperCDMS (Super Cryogenic Dark Matter Search) collaboration. Our group develops detectors to search for particle dark matter, and employs superconducting amplifiers, low-noise experimental methods, and novel data analysis techniques to improve the detectors' sensitivity. I completed my Ph.D. under Scott Oser's supervision at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, where I occasionally escaped from the lab to ski, bike, hike, and sail.
